About me

I'm a graduate UX designer based in Sydney. When I was little I was amazed at the technology of 3D-animated film productions which inspired me to go into this field. I've since went on to complete a degree of the Bachelor of Design Computing at the University of Sydney where I worked on projects across user experience design, interaction design and data visualisation.

In my free time I like to doodle a lot and have made stickers.

I now look forward to applying my knowledge to develop experiences with technology through people-centric lens.
