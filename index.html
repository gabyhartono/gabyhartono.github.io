About me

I work as a Product Designer at QuickaPay while studying Masters of Design at UNSW.

As a child I loved drawing but fell in love with design when I found out how 3D-animated films were made. I've since completed a degree of the Bachelor of Design Computing at the University of Sydney in 2020, where I worked on projects across user experience design, interaction design and data visualisation. I decided to take one step further by taking Master of Design at UNSW where I learn a lot about critical design while online learning.

In my free time I doodle a lot and have made some stickers.

UX is continuing to drive products and services today, and I'm excited to be developing our experiences with technology through a people-centric lens.
