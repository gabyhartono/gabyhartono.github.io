About me

I work as a Product Designer at RapidPay, a financial payments platform for the legal industry.

Like most people, I grew up drawing but fell in love with design when I found out how 3D-animated films were made. I've since done a Bachelor of Design Computing at the University of Sydney (2020), which introduced me to user experience and interaction design through design thinking methodologies. In 2022, I graduated from the Master of Design at UNSW where I navigated the critical state of design in today's world.

In my free time I doodle a lot and have made some stickers.

UX is continuing to drive products and services today, and I'm excited to develop our experiences with technology through a people-centric lens.
